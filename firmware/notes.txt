figure out how DHT22 transmission is done

RH = relative humidity
T = temperature

### transmission structure, total 40 bits

8 bits RH integral data
8 bits RH decimal data
8 bits integral T data
8 bits decimal T data
8 bit check-sum

the check sum should be the last 8 bits of sum:
8 bits RH integral data + 8 bits RH decimal data + 8 bits integral T data + 8 bits decimal T data

### transmission timing

MCU gives low start signal at least 1 ms
MCU waits 20-40 us on high for DHT22 response
DHT22 detects signal, send 80 us low signal as response
transmission begins, every bit transmission begins with a low of 50 us
next signal goes high, if for 26-28 us then bit is 0, if for 70 us then bit is 1
on to the next bit, total 40 bits
